What Makes Dark Shift Different

Solace Agent Mesh

Event-driven architecture with pub/sub backbone, orchestrator, and 5+ domain agents that react to chaos in real-time.

Chaos Cards

12 disruption scenarios — supplier delays, equipment failures, demand surges. Each ripples through the entire factory floor.

Priority Dial

Shift between Speed, Margin, Quality, and Green modes. Each choice instantly changes how every agent behaves.

Live KPIs

Track output, OTIF, margin, defect rate, energy, and carbon in real-time. Every decision moves the needle.

Swappable Scenarios

The bike factory is just one config file. Swap it for semiconductors, pharma, or any manufacturing line.

Zero Setup

Runs entirely in the browser. No server, no install. Open the HTML and play. Share a link and your audience is in.

Game Loop

Each session is 3-5 minutes. Endlessly replayable.

1

Factory hums

Stations run, AMRs zip, units flow through the line. Agents are quiet. You watch the KPIs.

2

Chaos strikes

A card flips. Supplier delay. Weld defect spike. Equipment failure. The event publishes to the agent mesh.

3

Agents propose

The orchestrator fans out to domain agents. Each proposes a fix with trade-offs. You pick one or approve all.

4

Factory adapts

Stations recover (or don't). KPIs shift. Your score reflects every decision's ripple effect.

5

Shift ends

After 5 minutes you get a grade and a shareable score. A+ Factory Lord — or back to training.
