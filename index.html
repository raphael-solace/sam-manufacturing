How it works

Agent Mesh

Event-driven pub/sub backbone with an orchestrator and 5 domain agents that react to disruptions in real-time.

Chaos Cards

12 disruption scenarios — supplier delays, equipment failures, demand surges. Each ripples through the factory.

Priority Dial

Shift between Speed, Margin, Quality, and Green modes. Each choice changes how every agent behaves.

Live KPIs

Track output, OTIF, margin, defect rate, energy, and carbon. Every decision moves the needle.

Swappable Scenarios

The bike factory is one config file. Swap it for semiconductors, pharma, or any manufacturing line.

Zero Setup

Runs entirely in the browser. No server, no install. Open the HTML and play.

Game loop

  1. Factory hums. Stations run, units flow through the line. Agents are quiet.
  2. Chaos strikes. A card flips. Supplier delay. Weld defect. Equipment failure.
  3. Agents propose. The orchestrator fans out. Each agent suggests a fix with trade-offs.
  4. You decide. Pick one proposal, approve all, or dismiss. Your call.
  5. Shift ends. After 5 minutes you get a grade. A+ Factory Lord — or back to training.
